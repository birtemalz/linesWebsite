    Narrative duration around 10 minutes
    Keywords: #DigitalPoetry, #P5JS, #Storytelling, #Perspectives
Any story told contains – and conceals – a plentitude of others that are not.
Sometimes we might walk through life starting to expect everyone around us to see the same – to tell the same story as we would. Lost in our own perspective... This is just a little narrative wake-up call questioning the power of who tells the story – and how. What would happen if we try to tell them from all sides?
